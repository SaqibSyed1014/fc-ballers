Player Resources

Everything FC Ballers players need for tryouts, training, communication, and expectations.

What to Bring to Tryouts

  • Shin guards (mandatory)
  • Cleats + running shoes
  • Black top & black shorts (clean look)
  • Water (minimum 32 oz)
  • Positive attitude + focus

Check-In Instructions

Arrive 15–20 minutes early.

Check in with staff at the field entrance. Have your receipt email ready if asked.

Player Standards

  • Respect teammates, opponents, referees, and staff.
  • Act like a professional—on and off the field.
  • Communicate promptly and clearly.
  • Train with intention, intensity, and discipline.
  • Represent the club with pride.

Required Gear

  • Cleats (firm ground)
  • Shin guards
  • Black training shorts
  • Black socks
  • Water bottle

How Players Are Evaluated

  • Technical quality (touch, passing, dribbling)
  • Decision-making + awareness
  • Work rate + intensity
  • Attitude + coachability
  • Movement + athletic ability

Communication Rules

  • Respond promptly to team messages.
  • Notify staff ASAP if late or unable to attend.
  • No negativity, arguing, or disrespect—ever.

What Happens After Tryouts

Players will be contacted via email regarding selection or next steps.

Selected players will receive onboarding details, team expectations, and uniform information.
