Teams

A Club Built to Compete — and Grow

FC Ballers is a semi-professional football club competing at a high level while building a long-term development structure. We are committed to creating clear pathways for men, women, and youth players who take the game seriously and want to progress.

We are not a recreational program. We are a performance-driven club grounded in discipline, creativity, and identity.

Men's First Team

Our Men's First Team represents the highest competitive level within FC Ballers.

This squad is built for:

  • High-level semi-professional competition
  • Structured training environments
  • Tactical discipline and accountability
  • Players focused on development, exposure, and winning

Roster spots are earned through performance, commitment, and fit — not reputation.

Women's Team (Planned)

FC Ballers is actively planning the launch of a Women's Team built with the same competitive standards and resources as the men's side.

Our goal is to:

  • Create a serious, well-supported competitive environment
  • Provide visibility and growth opportunities for women players
  • Build a culture rooted in respect, discipline, and ambition

Details on timelines and tryouts will be announced as the program develops.

B Team (Development Squad)

The B Team will serve as a competitive development pathway between grassroots football and the first team.

Designed for:

  • Players close to first-team level
  • Younger or late-entry players needing match experience
  • Internal competition and progression

Strong B Team performances will directly influence first-team opportunities.

Youth Academy (Planned)

The FC Ballers Youth Academy is a long-term project focused on player development, not shortcuts.

The academy will emphasize:

  • Technical foundation
  • Game intelligence and creativity
  • Discipline, habits, and professionalism
  • Clear pathways into senior teams

Our aim is to develop complete players — on and off the pitch.

Our Vision

FC Ballers is building a connected football system, not isolated teams.

Men. Women. Development. Youth.
One identity. One standard.

More details on tryouts, timelines, and expansion will be announced as each program launches.
