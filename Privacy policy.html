Privacy Policy

How FC Ballers collects, protects, and uses your information.

At FC Ballers, we value and protect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, register for tryouts, purchase merchandise, or interact with any of our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email, phone number, age, and details you submit during tryout registration.
  • Payment Details: Processed securely by third‑party providers (Stripe, PayPal). We never store full card numbers.
  • Order Information: Items purchased, shipping details, transaction details.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device type, and analytics data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Process tryout registrations and merchandise orders
  • Send confirmations, receipts, and important club updates
  • Improve website functionality and performance
  • Verify eligibility for participation
  • Enhance overall member and player experience

3. Protecting Your Information

We use strong, industry‑standard security measures to protect your data. All payments are encrypted and handled by trusted third‑party processors.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your information. We only share your data with:

  • Payment processors for secure transactions
  • Email services for delivering confirmations
  • Legal authorities if required by law

5. Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies to improve browsing experience and website performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 without parental consent. Parents or guardians must complete registrations for minors.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the information we hold about you
  • Request updates or corrections to your information
  • Request deletion of your information (where applicable)

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as FC Ballers grows. The latest version will always be available on this page.
