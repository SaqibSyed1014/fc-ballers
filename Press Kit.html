Club Overview

FC Ballers is a modern, competitive soccer club built around discipline, creativity, and a positionless playing identity. Based in the greater Chicagoland area, we focus on developing players with purpose and showcasing a bold new style of football.

Founded: 2025
Location: Chicago, Illinois
Division: Independent (Spring 2026 inaugural season)
