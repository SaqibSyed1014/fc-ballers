Refund Policy

Our official refund guidelines for tryouts, merchandise, and all FC Ballers transactions.

1. Tryout Registration Fees

All tryout fees are non-refundable.

Because tryout spots are limited and staffing, field rentals, and logistics are planned based on registrations, we cannot issue refunds for any reason, including:

  • Schedule conflicts
  • Injury or illness
  • Change of plans
  • Late arrival or no‑show

2. Merchandise Refunds

We offer refunds or exchanges on merchandise only if:

  • The item is unworn and in original condition
  • You contact us within 14 days of delivery
  • The item arrived damaged or incorrect

Customers are responsible for return shipping unless the product was defective or incorrect.

3. Digital Products

Any digital downloads or digital confirmations are non-refundable once delivered.

4. Processing Times

Refunds (where applicable) are processed within 5–10 business days after approval.
